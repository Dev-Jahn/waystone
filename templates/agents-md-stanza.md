## Waystone contract

- **Project frame**: `{BRIEF_PATH}` contains commitments, hypotheses, non-goals, and open questions. Preserve uncertainty; do not turn a hypothesis into a requirement.
- **Canonical surfaces**: use `waystone brief`, `waystone run`, `waystone review`, and `waystone status`.
- **Selected work**: use `waystone task list/show/add/set/drop`; task count is audit context, not progress authority.
- **Review**: claim, validation, disposition, and selected materialization are separate. A confirmed finding is not automatically a task.
- **Evidence boundaries**: worker output is a proposal; owner evidence and independent verification provide authority.
